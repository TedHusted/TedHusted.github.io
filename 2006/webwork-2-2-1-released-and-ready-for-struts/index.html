WebWork 2.2.1: Released and Ready for Struts!

2006-02-07
Event

The WebWork team announced a milestone release in the WebWork 2.2 series on Feburary 4. For details, see the WebWork announcement. A companion release of XWork 1.1 is also available.

Meanwhile, we have filed our merger proposal with the Apache Incubator PMC. The next step is to finish collecting Contributor License Agreements, and then we can bring the WebWork 2.x codebase into the incubator.
